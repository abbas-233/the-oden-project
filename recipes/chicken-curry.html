Chicken Curry

Description

A flavorful and aromatic chicken curry made with tender chicken pieces in a rich, spiced sauce. This dish is perfect served with rice or naan bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in curry powder, turmeric, cumin, and coriander.
  6. Add tomatoes and cook until they break down.
  7. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  8. Cover and cook for 20-25 minutes until chicken is tender.
  9. Season with salt to taste.
  10. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot.
