Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. It's creamy, rich, and absolutely delicious!

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 100g Parmigiano Reggiano cheese, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. While pasta cooks, fry the pancetta in a large pan until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated cheeses, and plenty of black pepper.
  4. Drain pasta, reserving some pasta water.
  5. Working quickly, add hot pasta to the pan with pancetta, remove from heat.
  6. Add the egg mixture, stirring constantly to create a creamy sauce. Add pasta water if needed.
  7. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper.
